Frequently Asked Questions

Are your services free of charge?

Our services are free because our goal is to encourage comunity growth

When is the right time to apply for a scholarship?

Different scholarships have different requirements and deadlines. Please refer to our Education Hub to identify a scholarship that aligns with your interest

How do I get funding for my organisation?

We will help you identify and apply for funding available as applicable to your organisation's cause. Please contact us to start the grant search process.
Note: Muzanganda does not provide funding to any organisation.

Do I need to bring a computer for the Tech training?

We provide computers during our skills training. Trainees are encouraged to bring a computer if they have one
